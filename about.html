About Me

I am an aspiring software developer, currently enrolled in an 18-month Software Development Learnership with GirlCode. I bring a background in Social Sciences, majoring in Psychology and Criminology, and a passion for technology’s potential to solve real-world problems. Growing up in rural areas and later moving to Diepsloot after the passing of my grandmother taught me resilience and gave me a purpose rooted in community upliftment.
I am especially drawn to the intersection of technology, behavioral science, and social impact. My journey includes furthering my skills in data science and web development, while exploring forensic psychology. Currently, I’m collaborating on a community-centered project to create a digital Z83 job application platform with fellow GirlCode learners. My goal is to become a versatile developer who builds inclusive, ethical, and impactful digital solutions.
